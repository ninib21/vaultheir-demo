## 2. Bank Financing Strategy

### 2.1 SBA Loan Programs

#### **SBA 7(a) Loan - General Small Business Loan**
- **Max Loan Amount**: $5M
- **Use Cases**: Working capital, equipment, real estate
- **Interest Rate**: Prime + 2.25-4.75%
- **Term**: 7-25 years
- **Requirements**:
  - Strong business plan ✓
  - Personal credit score >680
  - 2+ years in business (waiver for tech startups)
  - Collateral (may be waived for strong projections)

**Application Strategy:**
- Emphasize technology IP as collateral
- Highlight Hedera blockchain partnerships
- Show strong financial projections
- Present customer commitments (LOIs)

**Target Amount: $2M - $3M**

---

#### **SBA 504 Loan - Commercial Real Estate & Equipment**
- **Max Loan Amount**: $5.5M
- **Use Cases**: Real estate, major equipment
- **Interest Rate**: Fixed (below market)
- **Term**: 10-25 years
- **Requirements**: Similar to 7(a)

**Application Strategy:**
- If purchasing office space or equipment
- Can combine with 7(a) for larger needs

**Target Amount: $1M - $2M (if applicable)**

---

#### **SBA Microloan - Small Business Startups**
- **Max Loan Amount**: $50K
- **Use Cases**: Working capital, inventory
- **Interest Rate**: 8-13%
- **Term**: Up to 6 years
- **Requirements**: Minimal (good for startups)

**Application Strategy:**
- Quick funding for immediate needs
- Use as bridge financing

**Target Amount: $50K**

---

### 2.2 Traditional Bank Business Loans

#### **Business Term Loan**
- **Typical Amount**: $50K - $500K
- **Interest Rate**: 5-10% (varies by bank)
- **Term**: 1-5 years
- **Requirements**:
  - Business credit score >75
  - 2+ years in business (or strong projections)
  - Revenue >$100K/year (projected)
  - Personal guarantee

**Application Strategy:**
- Target regional banks (more flexible)
- Present ARR projections ($3M Year 1)
- Show customer pipeline
- Highlight intellectual property value

**Target Amount: $250K - $500K**

---

#### **Business Line of Credit**
- **Typical Amount**: $10K - $500K
- **Interest Rate**: Prime + 1-5%
- **Revolving**: Yes
- **Requirements**: Similar to term loan

**Application Strategy:**
- Use for working capital needs
- Flexible access to funds
- Lower initial requirements than term loans

**Target Amount: $200K - $500K**

---

### 2.3 Business Credit Cards

#### **Corporate Credit Cards**
- **Typical Limit**: $5K - $100K
- **Benefits**: 
  - Build business credit
  - Rewards/cashback
  - Expense management
- **Requirements**: Business EIN, business bank account

**Application Strategy:**
- Apply for multiple cards (spread risk)
- Use for operational expenses
- Pay in full monthly (build credit)

**Target Total Limits: $50K - $150K**

## 4. Alternative Financing Options

### 4.1 Revenue-Based Financing (RBF)

**Providers:**
- Capchase, Pipe, Lighter Capital
- **Typical Terms:**
  - 1.5-3x monthly revenue
  - 15-25% of monthly revenue until paid
  - 1-3 year terms
  - No equity dilution

**Application Strategy:**
- Use after $100K+ MRR achieved
- Faster than bank loans
- Good for growth capital

**Target Amount: $500K - $1.5M (Year 2)**

---

### 4.2 Equipment Financing

**Use Cases:**
- Servers, computers, office equipment
- **Typical Terms:**
  - 5-10% down payment
  - 3-7 year terms
  - Equipment as collateral

**Target Amount: $100K - $300K**

---

### 4.3 Invoice Financing / Factoring

**Use Cases:**
- Advance on enterprise contracts
- **Typical Terms:**
  - 80-90% of invoice value
  - 1-3% fee per month
  - Quick access to funds

**Application Strategy:**
- Use for enterprise sales (Year 2+)
- Bridge cash flow gaps
- Quick access to capital

---

### 4.4 Government Grants & Incentives

- **Phase I**: $50K - $250K (6 months)
- **Phase II**: $750K - $1.5M (2 years)
- **Eligibility**: US-based, <500 employees, tech focus

**Application Strategy:**

- Focus on IP protection technology
- Apply to multiple agencies (NSF, DoD, etc.)

**Target Amount: $250K - $500K**

---

## 5. Credit Building Strategy

### 5.1 Business Credit Profile

**Establish Business Credit:**
1. **Get EIN** (Employer Identification Number) ✓
2. **Open Business Bank Account** ✓
3. **Register with Business Credit Bureaus:**
   - Dun & Bradstreet (D-U-N-S number)
   - Experian Business
   - Equifax Business
4. **Get Business Credit Cards** (3-5 cards)
5. **Establish Trade Credit** (vendor accounts)
6. **Pay All Bills on Time** (critical)

**Target Timeline: 6-12 months to build strong profile**

---

### 5.2 Credit Score Targets

**Business Credit Score (D&B Paydex):**
- **Target**: 80+ (excellent)
- **Timeline**: 12-18 months
- **Actions**:
  - Pay all bills early (before due date)
  - Maintain low credit utilization (<30%)
  - Establish multiple credit relationships
  - Monitor credit reports regularly
